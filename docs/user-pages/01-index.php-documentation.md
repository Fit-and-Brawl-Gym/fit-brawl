## Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to create an account to book a day pass?**  
A: No! Day passes and single training sessions can be booked without an account.

**Q: What's the difference between the "Log in" button and "Non-Member Services"?**  
- **"Log in"**: For people interested in ongoing membership or availing day passes
- **"Non-Member Services"**: For people who want to try the gym first (one-time visits)

**Q: Can I see the non-member services without opening the popup?**  
A: Currently no. You must click the button to see pricing. There's a "View All Plans" link inside the popup that shows more details.

**Q: What happens if I'm logged in and visit this page?**  
A: You'll be immediately redirected to your member dashboard. You won't see the public homepage.
